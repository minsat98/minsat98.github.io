Who Are We?

Welcome to CyberClout. A Cyber Security company based in the heart of Wales, Cardiff. Here at CyberClout, we offer a range of services for both internal and external penetration testing. As technology evolves, so do the techniques to ensure data is kept secure. Which is why at CyberClout, we offer multiple packages which are tailored to value yours and your company’s individual needs – no matter how big or small.

Our pen test services come in several forms and will be both tailored and delivered by experienced security advisers. They are designed to reveal vulnerabilities within a target, and to manually verify whether these could be exploited by malicious threats. Our guarantee is to provide clients with the comprehensive remediation advice they need to improve their security situation.
